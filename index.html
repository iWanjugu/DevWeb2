About us

We make product ideas a reality. We’re a web and mobile development firm that prides ourselves on building high quality products with deep care for our clients’ success. At Moringa, we want to develop long-term relationships with those we work with.


Based in Kenya, we’re a blended and diverse team of creative thinkers from East Africa, America, and Europe. We’re here to show that East African developers can kick ass in tech too.

  • We primarily develop in Python/Django, JavaScript and Android
